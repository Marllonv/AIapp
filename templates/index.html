Prevention of Cardiovascular Diseases

Welcome to ROCA HEALTH!

Here, we use Artificial Intelligence techniques trained on real clinical data to estimate the risk of developing conditions such as hypertension, high cholesterol, diabetes, and obesity.

By filling out our form with simple information about your lifestyle and physical characteristics, our system processes the data and provides a personalized risk estimate. This technology does not replace a medical diagnosis, but it can help identify risk factors early and encourage healthier habits.

Your well-being starts with information.

Discover your risks and take care of your health today.

How We Work

Check out some of the development stages of our project.

01

Database

The database Smoking and Drinking Dataset with body signal was chosen to be used as the basis for our project.

02

Data Processing

Preparation and processing of data, using Google Collab to create graphs and CSV files used in the next step.

03

AI Training

Using the csv generated in the previous step to train artificial intelligence to perform our prediction.

04

Platform development

Development of the platform that explains a little about how it works and is ready to receive the data needed for forecasting.
